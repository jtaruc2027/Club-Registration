The Orchestra Club

Welcome to the renowned 'Orchestra Club!' This club is one with rich history, dating back to the 80s; we are considered to be one of the finest philharmonics in the nation. We've had the privilege of collaborating with renowned musicians like Leonard Bernstein, Yo-yo Ma, Lang Lang, John Williams, and more. Keep up with our activities and activities to come by signing up to our newsletter.

Anyone can join the orchestra. Auditions consist of preparing one (1) solo piece and other tests (see application details for list). Auditions are typically 5-10 minutes long and require semi-formal attire or a costume of any character (cosplay is not advised because it may get in the way, but it is funny). Our next cycle runs on April 28th - 32nd. We look forward to hearing from you!

What is the dress code for concerts?
This depends on the concert, with classical concerts requiring business casual or dressier attire. Other events have a more relaxed dress code.
Are food and drinks allowed?
No.
